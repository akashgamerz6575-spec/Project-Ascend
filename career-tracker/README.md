# Career Development & Professional Tracker

The centralized repository for professional milestones, credentials, applications, and interview records.

## Purpose
To compile and organize career preparation materials, verified certifications, resume variations, hackathon participation logs, and technical blog drafts.

## Subdirectory Structure
- **[Hackathons](file:///c:/Users/ManjuMJ/Documents/BEE/career-tracker/hackathons/README.md)**: Project logs and outcomes.
- **[Certifications](file:///c:/Users/ManjuMJ/Documents/BEE/career-tracker/certifications/README.md)**: Registry of verified technical credentials.
- **[Competitions](file:///c:/Users/ManjuMJ/Documents/BEE/career-tracker/competitions/README.md)**: Coding contest records (LeetCode/Codeforces).
- **[Internships](file:///c:/Users/ManjuMJ/Documents/BEE/career-tracker/internships/README.md)**: Log of professional experiences.
- **[Open Source](file:///c:/Users/ManjuMJ/Documents/BEE/career-tracker/open-source-contributions/README.md)**: Detailed reports of external contributions.
- **[Blogs](file:///c:/Users/ManjuMJ/Documents/BEE/career-tracker/blogs/README.md)**: Published technical write-ups.
- **[Research Papers](file:///c:/Users/ManjuMJ/Documents/BEE/career-tracker/research-papers/README.md)**: Academic research manuscripts.
- **[Interview Experiences](file:///c:/Users/ManjuMJ/Documents/BEE/career-tracker/interview-experiences/README.md)**: Deconstructed interview reviews.
- **[Resume Versions](file:///c:/Users/ManjuMJ/Documents/BEE/career-tracker/resume-versions/README.md)**: Chronological target resume logs.

## Usage Conventions
Copy the appropriate templates from `templates/career/` to these folders to track new career elements.

## Contribution Guidelines for Future Self
1. Log entries immediately following the close of an event (e.g., within 24 hours of completing an interview or competition).
2. Keep all credentials verifiable with ID checks or public links.
